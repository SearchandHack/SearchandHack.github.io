CrowdStrike resources

On July 19, 2024, a faulty update to CrowdStrike's security software caused widespread crashes of computers and virtual machines running Microsoft Windows. This incident led to what has been described as the largest outage in the history of information technology, affecting businesses and governments globally. The update triggered a "logic error" resulting in system crashes and the infamous "blue screen of death" (BSOD) on impacted systems.
